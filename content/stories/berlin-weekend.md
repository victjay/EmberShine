Winter Berlin is underrated. The cold keeps everyone in the bars and galleries rather than posing for photos in parks.

## Neukölln

Stayed in a flat above a döner shop. The host left a handwritten note about which bars to avoid and which tram to take to get to Tempelhof. Followed neither instruction. Found better options.

## Tempelhof

The old airport converted to a public park is one of the strangest and best public spaces I've seen. Flat, enormous, windswept. People kite-skate across it in winter. The runways are still there.

## Prenzlauer Berg

Sunday flea market at Mauerpark. Bought nothing useful. Watched a man sell someone a VHS tape of a 1994 football match for fifteen euros, both of them completely serious about it.
